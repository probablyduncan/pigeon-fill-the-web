to pigeon-fill the sky
made by duncan

This is a website about a book about pigeons.

The book is about doing something good, once, and trying to do it again, and forgetting how hard it was to do it the first time.

The website, I suppose, is about the same thing.

I made the book in 2024, and decided it needed a website almost exactly a year after it was finished.

When I made the book, I was thinking a lot about my old photography, and feeling like my newer work wasn't as good.

When I was making this website, I just wanted it over and done. I had a lot of fun, and I learned a lot, don't get me wrong, but I already did this project! And here I was, doing it all over again.

I guess I thought that adaptation was much less of a creative process than making something from scratch. That's certainly not true.
